My Upbringing

Hi my name is Matt and back when I was a just a young a lad I was an amazing soccer player! I kicked a lot of balls, ran a lot of miles, and drank a lot of gatorade! I was living the life! It all started when my best friend invited me to tryouts for his club team. Until this point I was just a typical recreational player only there for the snacks! However, my friend convinced me to tryout so I went! I didn't know this at the time but I SUCKED!! After tryouts I didn't make the team but I didn't know that nor did I care. I just kept going to practice with my friend and eventually one day my coach pulled me aside and said "You know Matt you never made the team..." Now I'm 13 years old so I said "oh ok" and that was it. My coach continued "...but you've shown me a good work ethic and quick improvements so I'd like to offer you a spot on the team". And the rest is history.
